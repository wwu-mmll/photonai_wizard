Optimization Design

{% endblock %} {% block step_description %}

Please specify the hyperparameter optimizer that PHOTON will use to find the best hyperparameters for your analysis. The nested cross-validation of your analysis is one of the most crucial parts of a Machine Learning analysis. Luckily, PHOTON will handle everything for you so that no validation mistakes can happen. Just specify an outer cross-validation that is used to obtain an unbiased performance estimate of your complete analysis and an inner cross-validation that is used to get a performance estimate for every hyperparameter configuration that PHOTON will test for you.
